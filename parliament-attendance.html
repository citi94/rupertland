Parliament attendance guidelines

What to expect

  • Sessions may start late or be rescheduled with short notice
  • Mobile phones must be switched off
  • Photography and recording are strictly prohibited
  • Food and drinks are not permitted in the gallery

Security requirements

  • All visitors must pass through security screening
  • Large bags and electronic devices may be refused entry
  • Photo ID is required and will be checked against booking details

Accessibility

  • Wheelchair accessible seating is available in the public gallery
  • Hearing loops are available in designated areas
  • Guide dogs are permitted
  • Large print materials can be provided on request
