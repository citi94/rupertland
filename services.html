Government Services

Access digital government services for citizens and businesses in the Principality of Rupertland. Most services are available 24/7 online with instant processing.

Digital First: All services are available online. You'll need your Rupertland Digital ID to access most services. Apply for Digital ID if you haven't already.

Popular Services

Rupertland Digital Identity Card

Apply for your official digital identity card. Required for accessing government services, voting, and proving citizenship.

  • Instant digital issuance
  • Biometric security features
  • Valid for 10 years
  • Cost: Free for citizens
Start application

Business and Commerce

  • Register a Business

    Start a new business in Rupertland with our streamlined registration process.

  • Tax Registration

    Register for business taxes and VAT.

  • Import/Export License

    Apply for licenses to import or export goods.

  • Professional Licenses

    Apply for professional practice licenses in regulated industries.

  • Property Registry Search

  • Building Permits

    Apply for permits to construct or modify buildings.

  • Rental Registration

    Register rental properties and tenancy agreements.

  • Housing Benefit

    Apply for assistance with housing costs.

Tax and Finance

    Submit your annual tax return online.
