Government department

Ministry of Finance

Responsible for government finances, taxation policy, economic planning, and financial services regulation across Rupertland.

Budget announcement

2025-26 Budget released. View full details.

What we do

The Ministry of Finance ensures sound financial management and economic stability through:

  • Tax policy development and collection
  • Government budget planning and monitoring
  • Economic policy and forecasting
  • Financial services regulation
  • Public debt management
  • Currency and monetary coordination
  • International financial relations

Our services

Taxation Services

Tax calculations, returns, and payment services.

Business Finance

Business taxation, VAT registration, and financial compliance.

Grants & Funding

Government grants, loans, and financial support programs.

Financial Regulation

Banking oversight, insurance regulation, and financial licensing.

Policies and priorities

Current priorities

  • Digital transformation of tax services
  • Support for post-pandemic economic recovery
  • Green finance and sustainable investment incentives
  • Financial inclusion and accessible banking services
  • Strengthened anti-money laundering measures

Strategic objectives (2024-2027)

  1. Achieve 95% digital adoption for tax services
  2. Reduce tax processing times by 40%
  3. Maintain government debt below 40% of GDP
  4. Increase financial services sector contribution to 12% of GDP
  5. Implement comprehensive open banking framework
