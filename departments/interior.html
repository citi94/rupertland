Ministry of Interior

Responsible for citizenship, immigration, policing, emergency services, and maintaining public safety and security across Rupertland.

Ministry update

New Digital ID cards now available. Apply today.

What we do

The Ministry of Interior protects citizens and maintains order through:

  • Citizenship and naturalization services
  • Immigration and border control
  • National police coordination
  • Emergency response and civil protection
  • Digital identity and population registry
  • Elections and democratic processes
  • Local government coordination

Our services

Citizenship Services

Apply for citizenship, certificates, and naturalization.

Immigration

Residence permits, visas, and immigration support.

Digital Identity

Digital ID cards and identity verification services.

Emergency Services

Emergency response, alerts, and civil protection.

Core divisions

  • Emergency Management Agency - Disaster response and civil protection

Policies and priorities

Current priorities

  • Modernization of immigration and citizenship processes
  • Enhanced digital identity security and privacy protection
  • Strengthened emergency preparedness and response capabilities
  • Improved coordination between national and local police forces
  • Streamlined service delivery through digital transformation

Strategic objectives (2024-2027)

  1. Achieve 95% digital service adoption for routine transactions
  2. Reduce immigration processing times by 50%
  3. Implement comprehensive national emergency response system
  4. Enhance cybersecurity for digital identity infrastructure
  5. Improve citizen satisfaction ratings to 90%+
