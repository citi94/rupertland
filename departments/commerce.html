Responsible for trade promotion, business development, innovation support, and economic growth initiatives across Rupertland.

What we do

The Ministry of Commerce drives economic prosperity and competitiveness through:

  • International trade promotion and facilitation
  • Business registration and regulatory support
  • Innovation and technology development
  • Export assistance and market access
  • Investment attraction and business incentives
  • Competition policy and consumer protection
  • Digital economy and e-commerce development

Policies and priorities

Current priorities

  • Digital transformation of business services
  • Green technology and sustainable business development
  • Post-Brexit trade relationship optimization
  • Small business support and entrepreneurship promotion
  • Artificial intelligence and emerging technology adoption

Strategic objectives (2024-2027)

  1. Increase total exports by 25% to RP 11.1 billion
  2. Achieve 90% digital service delivery for business processes
  3. Establish Rupertland as a leading fintech hub
  4. Reduce business registration time to under 24 hours
  5. Support creation of 50,000 new jobs in high-growth sectors
