Help using government services

Get help using Rupertland government services, websites, and digital tools.

Quick help

Phone support

  • General enquiries: 0800-GOVERNMENT (24/7)
  • Technical support: 0800-DIGITAL (Mon-Fri 8am-6pm)
  • Emergency services: 911

Online help

  • Citizen Portal help centre
  • Find your local service centre
  • Live chat support (available 9am-5pm)

Common questions

How do I register for government services?

Use your Digital Citizen Portal to access most government services online. You'll need your citizenship ID or residence permit to create an account.

I forgot my Citizen Portal password

Visit the password reset page or call 0800-DIGITAL for assistance. You'll need to verify your identity using personal information.

How long do government services take?

Most online services are processed within 2-5 business days. Emergency services are available 24/7. Check specific service pages for detailed timeframes.

Can I get help in other languages?

Government services are available in English, French, Spanish, and Indigenous Rupertish. Translation services available for other languages by appointment.
