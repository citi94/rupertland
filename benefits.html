Benefits and financial support

The Principality of Rupertland provides comprehensive financial support and benefits to eligible citizens through various government programs.

Universal Benefits

Rupertland Citizen's Allowance

Monthly payment of RP$400 to all citizens aged 18 and over. No means test required. Automatically paid into your designated bank account on the 15th of each month.

  • Must be a registered citizen of Rupertland
  • Must have permanent address in the principality
  • Automatic enrollment at age 18

Child Benefit

RP$120 per month for each child under 16, or under 20 if in approved education or training.

Means-tested Benefits

Housing Support

Help with rent and mortgage payments for those on low incomes. Covers up to 80% of housing costs.

Educational Support Grant

Up to RP$2,000 per year for approved courses at Rupertland institutions. Additional RP$500 monthly living allowance for full-time students.

Family Income Supplement

Additional support for families with household income below RP$30,000 annually. Payment varies based on family size and circumstances.

Disability and Health Benefits

Disability Living Allowance

RP$300-800 monthly depending on assessment level. Covers additional costs of living with disability.

Carer's Allowance

RP$250 monthly for those providing substantial care to family members or friends.

Employment Support

Job Seeker's Allowance

RP$600 monthly for up to 12 months while actively seeking employment. Includes access to job training programs.

Start-up Business Grant

One-time grant of up to RP$5,000 for new business ventures. Includes mentorship and business development support.

How to apply

Most benefits can be applied for online through your Digital Citizen Portal. For assistance, visit any Citizens Service Centre or call 0800-BENEFITS.

Benefit rates for 2024

All benefit rates are reviewed annually and increased in line with the Cost of Living Index.

! Warning Benefit fraud is a serious offense. Providing false information may result in prosecution and repayment of benefits.
