Government response

How to participate

  1. Review the consultation document and supporting materials
  2. Consider the questions being asked
  3. Submit your response online or by post
  4. Encourage others to participate

Who can participate

  • Citizens and residents of Rupertland
  • Businesses operating in Rupertland
  • Organizations and representative groups
  • Academic institutions and researchers
  • International stakeholders (where relevant)

Types of responses we accept

  • Online form submissions
  • Email responses
  • Postal submissions
  • Video submissions (for accessibility)
  • Group or organizational responses
All consultation responses are public unless you request confidentiality. Personal information is handled according to our privacy policy.

What happens after consultation closes

  1. We analyze all responses received
  2. Key themes and recommendations are identified
  3. Government response is prepared
  4. Policy decisions are made and communicated
  5. Implementation timeline is published

Government responses are typically published within 12 weeks of consultation closure.
