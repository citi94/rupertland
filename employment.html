Employment support

Jobseeker's allowance

Financial support while you look for work. You may be eligible if you're:

  • Unemployed or working less than 16 hours per week
  • Actively seeking employment
  • A citizen or legal resident of Rupertland
  • Aged 18-65

Minimum wage rates (2024-25)

Category Hourly rate
Adult (23+) RP 15.50
Young adult (18-22) RP 12.25

Know your rights

  • 28 days paid annual leave (including public holidays)
  • Parental leave and pay
  • Protection against discrimination
  • Safe working conditions
  • Right to join a trade union
