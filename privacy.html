Privacy policy

This privacy policy explains how the Government of the Principality of Rupertland collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: 1 January 2024

Information we collect

Personal information

We collect personal information when you:

  • Apply for government services
  • Register for the Citizen Portal
  • Contact government departments
  • Participate in consultations
  • Visit government websites

Types of information

This may include:

  • Name, address, and contact details
  • Date of birth and citizenship information
  • Employment and financial information
  • Health information (for healthcare services)
  • Website usage data and cookies

How we use your information

Service delivery

We use your information to:

  • Provide government services you request
  • Process applications and payments
  • Verify your identity and eligibility
  • Communicate about your services
  • Improve service quality

Legal obligations

We may use information to:

  • Comply with legal requirements
  • Prevent and detect fraud
  • Conduct research and policy development
  • Maintain public health and safety

Information sharing

Within government

We may share information between government departments when necessary to provide services or fulfill legal obligations. This includes:

  • Coordinating related services
  • Verifying information accuracy
  • Preventing duplicate applications
  • Conducting audits and investigations

External sharing

We only share information with external organizations when:

  • You have given consent
  • Required by law
  • Necessary for service delivery
  • In emergency situations

Your rights

Access to information

You have the right to:

  • Request copies of your personal information
  • Know how your information is being used
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Making requests

To access or correct your information:

  • Use the Citizen Portal for most services
  • Contact the relevant government department directly
  • Submit a formal access request under privacy legislation

Data security

Protection measures

We protect your information through:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure government data centres
  • Regular security audits and testing
  • Staff training on privacy protection
  • Access controls and monitoring

Data retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing ongoing services
  • Meeting legal requirements
  • Historical and archival purposes

Cookies and website data

How we use cookies

Government websites use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Keep you signed in to services
  • Understand how you use our websites
  • Improve website performance

See our cookies policy for more details.

Children's privacy

We take special care with information about children under 16. Parental consent is required for most online services, and we limit data collection to what is necessary for the service.

International data transfers

Your information is stored within Rupertland. Any transfers outside the principality require appropriate safeguards and, where required, your explicit consent.

Privacy complaints

Making a complaint

If you're concerned about how your information is handled:

  1. Contact the relevant government department first
  2. If not satisfied, contact the Privacy Commissioner
  3. You may also contact the Ombudsman

Privacy Commissioner

Phone: 0800-PRIVACY-RP
Email: privacy@gov.rp
Office: Privacy Commissioner, Government Complex

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. For significant changes, we'll provide additional notice.

Contact us

For privacy questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@gov.rp
  • Phone: 0800-PRIVACY
  • Mail: Privacy Office, Government Complex, Capital District
