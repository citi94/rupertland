Tax rates for 2024-25

Personal income tax

Income band Tax rate
Up to RP 15,000 0% (tax-free allowance)
RP 15,001 - RP 50,000 20%
RP 50,001 - RP 125,000 35%
Over RP 125,000 45%

Business and corporate tax

  • Small business rate (up to RP 500,000 profit): 15%
  • Standard corporate rate: 25%
  • Capital gains tax: 20%
  • Dividend tax: 10%

Important dates

Date Deadline
31 January Self-assessment tax returns
31 March Corporate tax returns
31 July First payment on account
31 January Final payment and second payment on account
