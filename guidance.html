Enforcement and penalties

Rupertland takes a graduated approach to regulatory enforcement, with education and support preferred over penalties where possible. However, serious violations may result in significant fines, license suspension, or prosecution.

Reporting violations

Citizens can report suspected regulatory violations confidentially:

  • Environmental violations: 0800-ENVIRO-CRIME
  • Workplace safety issues: 0800-WORK-SAFE
  • Consumer protection: 0800-CONSUMER
  • Financial misconduct: 0800-FINANCIAL
