Ministers and Officials

Meet the government ministers and senior officials who lead the Principality of Rupertland and oversee the delivery of public services and policy implementation.

Head of State

His Serene Highness Prince Rupert Harding

Head of State and Constitutional Monarch

Prince Rupert founded the Principality of Rupertland in 2018 and serves as constitutional monarch. A former technology executive and environmental advocate, he brings expertise in digital innovation and sustainable development to his role as Head of State.

Background

  • Education: PhD in Environmental Science, MIT; MBA from Stanford
  • Previous roles: Chief Technology Officer at GreenTech Solutions; UN Climate Advisor
  • Founded: Principality of Rupertland in 2018
  • Focus areas: Environmental sustainability, digital governance, citizen welfare

Cabinet Secretary

Sir Robert Taylor

  • Cabinet Secretary

Cabinet Secretary since January 2019
Most senior civil servant, responsible for coordinating government policy across departments and supporting the Prime Minister and Cabinet in decision-making.

Background: 25 years in civil service, former Permanent Secretary in UK government

Parliamentary Leadership

Speaker of Parliament

The Hon. Patricia Williams MP

  • Speaker

Speaker of Parliament since May 2024
Presides over parliamentary sessions, maintains order during debates, and ensures democratic procedures are followed. Former barrister with expertise in constitutional law.

Background: LLB Law (Cambridge), former barrister and constitutional law expert

Judicial Leadership

The Rt Hon. Justice Anna Schmidt

  • Chief Justice

Chief Justice since June 2019
Head of the Supreme Court of Rupertland and senior judge in the judicial system. Appointed by Prince Rupert on advice of Parliament for a 10-year term.

Responsibilities: Supreme Court leadership, judicial administration, constitutional interpretation
Background: LLM Constitutional Law, 20 years judicial experience
