What you'll need

For all business types

  • Proposed business name (we'll check availability)
  • Business address in Rupertland
  • Description of business activities
  • Contact details
  • Your Digital ID for online applications

Additional requirements by business type

Limited company requirements
  • Articles of incorporation
  • Details of directors and shareholders
  • Registered office address
  • Share capital information
  • Statutory declaration of compliance
Partnership requirements
  • Partnership agreement
  • Details of all partners
  • Profit sharing arrangements
  • Management responsibilities

Business name rules

Your business name must:

  • Be unique and not already in use
  • Not contain offensive or misleading words
  • Not suggest government association (unless approved)
  • Include appropriate suffix (Ltd, LLC, Partnership, etc.)
Use our business name checker to verify availability before submitting your application.

Registration process

  1. Choose your business structure
  2. Check business name availability
  3. Prepare required documents
  4. Complete online application or download forms
  5. Pay registration fee
  6. Submit application
  7. Receive certificate of incorporation
  8. Register for taxes and obtain licenses

After registration

Immediate next steps

Tax registration

Register for business taxes and VAT (if applicable).

Business licenses

Apply for industry-specific licenses and permits.

Ongoing obligations

  • File annual returns
  • Maintain statutory registers
  • Submit financial statements
  • Notify changes to company details
  • Pay annual registration fees

Foreign business registration

Foreign companies wanting to operate in Rupertland must register as:

  • Branch office (for temporary operations)
  • Subsidiary company (for permanent establishment)
  • Representative office (for non-commercial activities)
