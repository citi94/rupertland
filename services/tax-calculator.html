Current tax rates (2024-25)

Personal income tax bands

Income band Tax rate
RP 0 - RP 15,000 0% (Personal allowance)
RP 15,001 - RP 50,000 20% (Basic rate)
RP 50,001 - RP 125,000 35% (Higher rate)
Over RP 125,000 45% (Additional rate)

Tax allowances and reliefs

  • Personal allowance: RP 15,000 (full allowance)
  • Spouse allowance: RP 3,000 (if spouse not working)
  • Child allowance: RP 2,500 per child under 18
  • Pension contribution relief: Up to RP 40,000 annually
  • Charitable donation relief: 25% tax relief on donations
