Calculate your Income Tax

Use this service to calculate your Income Tax for the 2024 to 2025 tax year (6 April 2024 to 5 April 2025).

! Warning This calculator gives you an estimate. Your actual tax may be different.

Before you start

You'll need to know:

  • your total income for the tax year
  • any pension contributions you've made
  • any charitable donations you've made
  • whether you have dependents

This calculator is for people living in Rupertland for tax purposes. Different rules may apply if you live elsewhere.

Enter your total gross income before tax for the 2024-25 tax year. Include salary, bonuses, and any other taxable income.
This affects how much tax you pay and which allowances you can claim.

Deductions and reliefs (optional)

Total amount you've paid into pension schemes this tax year
Donations made through Gift Aid to registered charities

Income Tax rates and bands

Tax year 2024 to 2025

Personal allowances and rates
Band Taxable income Tax rate
Personal allowance Up to RP 15,000 0%
Basic rate RP 15,001 to RP 50,000 20%
Higher rate RP 50,001 to RP 125,000 35%
Additional rate Over RP 125,000 45%

Additional allowances

  • Spouse allowance: RP 3,000 (if spouse is not working)
  • Child allowance: RP 2,500 per child under 18
  • Pension relief: Tax relief on contributions up to RP 40,000

What this calculator includes

  • Income Tax on earnings, pensions and savings
  • personal allowances and reliefs
  • pension contribution relief
  • charitable donation relief
  • dependent allowances

What this calculator does not include

  • National Insurance contributions
  • tax on dividends
  • Capital Gains Tax
  • Inheritance Tax
  • business rates or VAT
  • other taxes and duties

For a full tax calculation including all taxes and National Insurance, complete your annual tax return or speak to a qualified tax advisor.
