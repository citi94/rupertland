Property transactions

Buying or selling property

When you buy or sell property in Rupertland, you must:

  1. Obtain an official copy of the title register
  2. Complete property transfer forms
  3. Pay stamp duty (if applicable)
  4. Register the transfer with the Land Registry
  5. Update ownership records
Use a qualified conveyancer or solicitor for property transactions. Find registered conveyancers.

Stamp duty rates

Property value Stamp duty rate
Up to RP 250,000 0%
RP 250,001 - RP 500,000 3%
RP 500,001 - RP 1,000,000 5%
Over RP 1,000,000 8%

New property registration

Register newly constructed properties or subdivided land:

Requirements for new registration

  • Building completion certificate
  • Planning permission documentation
  • Survey plans and measurements
  • Proof of ownership of parent land
  • Environmental compliance certificates

Registration fee: RP 300 for residential properties, RP 500 for commercial
