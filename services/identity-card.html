Rupertland Digital Identity Card

Your secure digital identity for accessing all government services. One card, all services - both online and in person.

New feature

Digital ID cards now include contactless payment

Pay for government services instantly with your Digital ID card. No cash or separate cards needed.

Apply for your Digital ID card

All citizens and legal residents of Rupertland are eligible for a Digital Identity Card. This replaces previous paper-based identity documents.

Processing time: 5-7 working days for standard applications. Express service available for urgent applications (additional fee applies).
Apply for Digital ID card

What you'll need to apply

  • Valid passport or current identity document
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, or tenancy agreement)
  • Birth certificate (if born in Rupertland)
  • Recent passport-style photograph
  • Application fee: RP 75 (standard) or RP 150 (express)

Application fees

Service Fee Processing time
Standard application RP 75 5-7 working days
Express application RP 150 1-2 working days
Replacement card RP 50 3-5 working days
Card renewal RP 60 5-7 working days

What you can do with your Digital ID

Online services

  • Access all government services with secure login
  • File tax returns and check refund status
  • Apply for benefits and financial support
  • Register life events (births, deaths, marriages)
  • Apply for business licenses and permits
  • Book appointments with government departments
  • Participate in government consultations and voting

In-person services

  • Prove your identity at government offices
  • Access healthcare services
  • Use public transport and facilities
  • Make contactless payments for government services
  • Access emergency services with medical information

Security features

  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint and facial recognition)
  • Encrypted chip technology
  • Contactless NFC capability
  • Tamper-evident holographic security features
  • Two-factor authentication for online access

Card validity and renewal

Digital ID cards are valid for:

  • 10 years for adults (18+)
  • 5 years for young people (16-17)
  • 3 years for children (under 16)

You'll receive renewal reminders 3 months before your card expires. Renew your Digital ID card

Privacy and data protection

Your Digital ID is protected by the highest security standards:

  • All data is encrypted and stored in secure government facilities
  • You control what information is shared and with whom
  • Usage logs are available for your review
  • Full compliance with data protection regulations
  • Regular security audits and updates

Read our privacy policy for full details on how we protect your personal information.

! Warning Keep your Digital ID card safe. Report lost or stolen cards immediately to prevent fraud.
