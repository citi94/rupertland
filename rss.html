RSS Feeds

Subscribe to RSS feeds to automatically receive updates from Rupertland government websites in your preferred RSS reader or news aggregator.

What is RSS? RSS (Really Simple Syndication) allows you to receive automatic updates from websites without visiting them directly. You'll need an RSS reader app or browser extension.

How to Use RSS Feeds

Popular RSS Readers

  • Desktop: Thunderbird, Outlook, Apple Mail
  • Web-based: Feedly, Inoreader, The Old Reader
  • Mobile: Feedly app, RSS Reader apps
  • Browser extensions: RSS Feed Reader for Chrome, Firefox

Setting Up RSS

  1. Choose an RSS reader application or service
  2. Copy the feed URL from the feeds listed above
  3. Add the feed URL to your RSS reader
  4. Configure update frequency (recommended: check every hour)
  5. Organize feeds into folders by topic if desired

Feed Information

Technical Details

  • Format: RSS 2.0 and Atom 1.0 supported
  • Encoding: UTF-8
  • Update frequency: Varies by feed (hourly to daily)
  • Item retention: 25-50 most recent items per feed
  • Full content: Complete article text included in most feeds

Feed Policies

  • RSS feeds are provided free of charge for personal and non-commercial use
  • Commercial use requires permission - contact feeds@gov.rp
  • Feeds may be discontinued or modified with 30 days notice
  • No guaranteed service level - feeds provided "as is"
  • Rate limiting applies to prevent abuse
