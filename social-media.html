Social Media Guidelines

Engaging with Government

  • Questions: Tag us or use #AskRupertland for policy questions
  • Service issues: DM @RupertlandGov for assistance with government services
  • Complaints: Use official channels or visit feedback page
  • Emergency: Call 999 - do not use social media for emergencies

Community Standards

We encourage respectful dialogue and community engagement. Our social media accounts are moderated to ensure constructive conversations.

  • Be respectful and constructive in comments
  • No hate speech, harassment, or discrimination
  • Stay on topic and relevant to posts
  • No spam or promotional content
  • Fact-check information before sharing

Social Media Support

Response Times

  • General enquiries: Within 2 hours (business hours)
  • Service issues: Within 1 hour (business hours)
  • Complex questions: Within 24 hours
  • Weekend/holidays: Next business day

Business Hours

Our social media team monitors accounts during:

  • Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (CET)
  • Emergency monitoring: 24/7 for urgent issues
  • Major events: Extended hours during special events

Report Fake Accounts

Security notice: Be aware of fake government accounts spreading misinformation. Always verify accounts have official verification badges.

If you encounter fake accounts impersonating Rupertland government:

  • Report to the social media platform
  • Email: security@gov.rp
  • Include screenshots and account links
  • Do not engage with suspicious accounts
