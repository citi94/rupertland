Rupertland Digital Identity

Your secure digital identity for accessing all government services online. One account, all services.

Important

New security features available - update your account settings for enhanced protection.

What you can do with your Digital ID

  • Apply for benefits and financial assistance
  • Register births, deaths, and marriages
  • File tax returns and check refund status
  • Register businesses and professional licenses
  • Book appointments with government services
  • Access healthcare records and prescriptions
  • Apply for passports and travel documents
  • Participate in government consultations

Apply for Digital ID

You need to be a citizen or legal resident of Rupertland to apply for a Digital ID.

What you'll need

  • Proof of identity (passport, national ID card, or birth certificate)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, or rental agreement)
  • Your National Insurance number
  • A valid email address
  • UK mobile phone number
Application processing time: 5-10 working days for standard applications. Express service available for urgent cases (additional fee applies).

Manage your existing Digital ID

If you already have a Digital ID, you can:

  • Update your personal information
  • Change your password or security settings
  • View your service history
  • Download certificates and documents
  • Manage privacy preferences

Security and privacy

Your Digital ID uses the highest security standards:

  • Multi-factor authentication
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • GDPR compliant data protection
  • Secure government data centres

Read our privacy policy for full details on how we protect your information.

Help and support

If you need help with your Digital ID:

  • Phone: 0800-DIGITAL-ID (0800-344-4825)
  • Email: support@digital.gov.rp
  • Live chat: Available 8am-6pm, Monday to Friday
  • In person: Visit any Citizen Service Centre
! Warning Never share your Digital ID login details with anyone. Government will never ask for your password by phone or email.
