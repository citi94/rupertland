Policy development process

How policy is made

Rupertland follows a transparent, evidence-based approach to policy development:

  1. Issue identification and research
  2. Stakeholder consultation and public engagement
  3. Options analysis and impact assessment
  4. Cabinet consideration and approval
  5. Legislative Assembly review (if required)
  6. Implementation and monitoring
  7. Evaluation and review

Public consultation

Citizens and stakeholders can participate in policy development through:

  • Public consultation papers and surveys
  • Community forums and town halls
  • Online engagement platforms
  • Advisory committees and working groups
  • Parliamentary committee hearings
